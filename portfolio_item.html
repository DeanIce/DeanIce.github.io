A Unity Game: AstroRogue

Unity, Scrum management, 3D Game design

AstroRogue is a rouge-like 3rd person shooter with inspiration from Risk of Rain 2 and Super Mario Galaxy. As a part of a team, I developed procedural level genertation, programmed and implemented the UI with Unity's new UI framework, helped design custom planetary gravity and more.

My team and I were in charge of setting our own goals and tracking progress using scrum framework. I helped create bi-weekly presentations showcasing progress and displaying some of our metrics, like a burndown chart. As this was a professionally led development project, we had extensive planning meetings and compiled detailed information aobut progress and goals.
